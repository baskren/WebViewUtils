For Lateral Loading scenarios, the Dowel Bearing Strength (DBS) of a fastener is a function of the diameter of the fastener body. A reduced body fastener continues the root diameter beyond the fasteners threads, through the length of the body. Most importantly, the root diameter traverses the shear plane, decreasing the Dowel Bearing Strength.
